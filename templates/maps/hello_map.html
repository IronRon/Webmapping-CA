Explore Ireland's Car Washes
How to Use
User Mode:
  • Click anywhere on the map to find the nearest car wash.
  • See a list of the 10 closest car washes in the sidebar.
Business Mode:
  • Counties are color-coded by car wash density: Red = more car washes, Green = fewer car washes.
  • Click to get recommendations for new car wash locations.
  • Switch between County Recommendation and Circle Recommendation using the toggle below.
Mode
Business Mode Parameters
County Recommendation:
  • Click a county to analyze car wash coverage and get recommended sites based on population and distance from existing car washes.
Circle Recommendation:
  • Set the center and radius for a custom area on the map.
  • Get recommended locations for new car washes within the selected circle.
Polygon Recommendation:
  • Click on the map to add points.
  • Click “Finish Polygon” when done.
